§ 01THE PROBLEM
The Incomplete Game

Most traders only see a third of the market.

A screener tells you which stocks moved. A fundamentals site tells you what they earn. Neither tells you whether you should be buying at all.

Studies show roughly 30% of a stock's move is explained by overall market direction, 30% by sector performance, and only 40% is stock-specific. That means 60% of what happens to your trade is determined by forces invisible to any stock-level tool. — STAGE ANALYSIS RESEARCH · Q-BRAM METHODOLOGY
CONVENTIONAL TOOLS

What price tells you

The last hour. Where a stock closed versus yesterday. Everything a chart already shows.

BREADTH ANALYSIS

What breadth tells you

Where the next hour is heading. Whether the rally has conviction, or only ten stocks carrying the index.

REGIME INTELLIGENCE

What regime tells you

Whether to be in the game at all. The one question no screener answers — the one that determines everything.

Q.01 What is Q-BRAM — and what makes it different from every other indicator? +

Q-BRAM (Quantitative Breadth Regime Analysis Model) is a proprietary 5-regime market classifier developed and backtested on 19 years of NIFTY 500 data. Unlike a single indicator, it combines 50+ breadth metrics — A/D line, DMA crossings, new highs/lows, thrust indicators — into a single composite score. The insight: signals in Expansion show a 61.8% win rate vs 34% in Distribution. Most traders use price alone. Q-BRAM reads the health of the entire market.

Q.02 Is this only useful for Indian markets? +

The platform is India-first — full NSE/BSE coverage, NIFTY 500 universe, SEBI PIT insider filings, and FII/DII institutional data. US market (Russell 3000) is partially integrated and expanding. Indian systematic traders get maximum value today.

Q.03 How does this compare to Tickertape, Screener.in, or Chartink? +

Those tools are stock-level screeners and fundamental databases. Quantum Breadth 360 is a market-breadth intelligence platform — it tells you the health of the entire market and where institutional money is flowing before you look at individual stocks. The Trading Journal also ties every trade to its regime automatically, which no other platform in India offers.

Q.06 Is this suitable for intraday traders? +

Quantum Breadth 360 is optimised for swing and positional traders with 2-day to 8-week holding periods — following Minervini SEPA, O'Neil CAN SLIM, Qullamaggie momentum, and Weinstein Stage Analysis. Breadth data is most valuable over multi-day horizons. Intraday traders will find limited utility here.
