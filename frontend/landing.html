Frequently Asked
What is Q-BRAM and why does it matter?
Q-BRAM (Quantitative Breadth Regime Analysis Model) is a proprietary market classification framework backtested on 19 years of NIFTY 500 data. It classifies the market into 5 regimes — Panic, Distribution, Transition, Accumulation, and Expansion — giving you a single score to trade from. Our backtests show a 61.8% win rate on signals taken in Expansion regime vs 34% in Distribution.
Is this only for Indian markets?
The platform is India-first (NSE/BSE) with full coverage of NIFTY 500 stocks, NSE sectors, FII/DII data, and SEBI PIT insider filings. US market (Russell 3000) data is partially integrated and expanding. Indian traders will get the most value from the platform today.
How is this different from Tickertape or Screener.in?
Those tools are fundamental screeners. Quantum Breadth 360 is a market-breadth intelligence platform — it tells you the health of the overall market, where institutional money is flowing, and which sectors are leading before you even look at individual stocks. The Trading Journal then ties your trades back to regime context — something no other platform does.
What happens after the 14-day Pro trial?
After your trial, you'll be downgraded to the Explorer (free) tier automatically — no charges, no surprises. Your data and trades are preserved. You can upgrade to Pro at any time from within the app to restore full access.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription?
Yes, anytime from the Billing section inside your account. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep full Pro access until then. No partial refunds, but no lock-in either.
Is my data safe?
All data is stored securely. We do not sell your trading data or share it with third parties. Payments are handled entirely by Razorpay — we never see your card details.
